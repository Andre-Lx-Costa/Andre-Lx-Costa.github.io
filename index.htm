About Me

Product Owner with a background in software engineering, bringing a deep understanding of both product direction and technical execution.
I bring a rare combination of hands on engineering experience and a people first mindset, grounded in a deep understanding of what teams build, how they build it, and what tends to slow them down. This perspective helps me translate ideas into clear requirements, make faster and better informed trade off decisions, and keep engineers and stakeholders aligned around outcomes that matter. Passionate, collaborative, and naturally curious, I focus on creating clarity, momentum, and team environments where delivery feels smooth and intentional rather than forced.
